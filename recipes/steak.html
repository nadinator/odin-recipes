Steak

Description

Keep it simple. You don't have to pretend to like it medium-rare or medium: medium-well is just fine.

Ingredients

  • Your preference of steak. Ribeye is king though.
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • Rosemary
  • Garlic (3-4) cloves for each steak piece
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Dijon Mustard
